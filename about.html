Denzel Valentine is currently a shooting guard and small forward for the Chicago Bulls whose passion for music has followed him throughout his NBA career. As an accomplished player, the Michigan Native has added music to his agenda delegating his free time during the pandemic to work on his music. Denzel’s inclination to music dates back to his upbringing in Lansing, Michigan. The 26-year-old Bulls player cites his father as the catalyst of his music career, as they frequently dissected hip-hop’s infamous lyricism together at the time. Denzel's latest ep "517 : Made Me" features Rockie Fresh, YP, Saint James, Brandon Fox, Tasha Catour, Epidemic, KG, Stef! & more Denzel is currently releasing his music on UnitedMasters distribution.
